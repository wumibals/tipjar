# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment. All contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in all communications.
- Accept constructive feedback graciously.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Show empathy and kindness toward other contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable in this project:

- Disrespectful, dismissive, or hostile comments.
- Personal attacks or insults directed at other contributors.
- Publishing private information of others without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a collaborative setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers directly. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and any other official communication channels.
